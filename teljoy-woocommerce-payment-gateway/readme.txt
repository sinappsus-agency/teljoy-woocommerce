This is the official WooCommerce extension to process payments using the South African Teljoy payments provider.

== Description ==

The Teljoy extension for WooCommerce enables you to accept once off payments, including Teljoy Rent To buy models, via one of South Africaâ€™s most trusted providers.

= Why choose Teljoy? =

Teljoy gives your customers more flexibility by allowing them to rent the product and return it when they desired while you get settled in full. Beyond Settling once off payments

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this require a Teljoy merchant account? =

Yes! A Teljoy merchant account, merchant key and merchant ID are required for this gateway to function.

= Does this require an SSL certificate? =

An SSL certificate is recommended for additional safety and security for your customers.
